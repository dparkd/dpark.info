The industry prefers the term product designer, but that carries an expectation to be a designer who optimizes for hyper-growth. This model of creation punishes creativity and exploration. I don't want to be beholden to such rigid structures.

Instead I aim to enter spaces with the intent to explore interfaces as an artist. If you’d like to invite me to your space as a designer-in-residence, email me at email@dpark.info
